## 1. Introduction to Salesforce

* Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform.
* It helps companies manage customers, sales, and services.
* Businesses use Salesforce to automate workflows and improve productivity.

---

# 2. What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

CRM helps organizations:

* Store customer information
* Track customer interactions
* Improve customer service
* Manage sales processes

Examples:

* Managing customer leads
* Tracking sales
* Handling support requests

# 4. Objects in Salesforce

Objects are database tables used to store data.

## Types of Objects

### Standard Objects

Provided by Salesforce.

Examples:

* Account
* Contact
* Lead

### Custom Objects

Created according to business needs.

Examples:

* Property
* Offer
* Student

---

# 5. Records and Fields

## Records

A single row of data inside an object.

Example:

* One student details record

## Fields

Used to store information inside records.

Examples:

* Name
* Email
* Phone Number
* Address

---

# 6. Relationships in Salesforce

Relationships connect objects together.

## Lookup Relationship

* Loose connection between objects
* Child record can exist independently

## Master-Detail Relationship

* Strong parent-child relationship
* Child record depends on parent record
